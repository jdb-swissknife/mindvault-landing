## Logo Concepts

### Concept 1: "Vault Brain"
- Icon: Stylized vault ring with neural network pathways branching from center
- Story: Security + Intelligence. Your data is safe, and the AI thinks.
- Files: `logo-1-icon.svg`, `logo-1-full.svg`

### Concept 2: "MV Shield" (RECOMMENDED)
- Icon: Shield containing an MV monogram. White M, blue V.
- Story: Protection + Identity. We guard your business with AI.
- Files: `logo-2-icon.svg`, `logo-2-full.svg`
- Why recommended: Cleanest at small sizes (favicon, app icon). Most versatile. Shield = trust. MV monogram = memorable. Works on dark/light backgrounds.

### Concept 3: "Node Network"
- Icon: Connected nodes forming an implied M shape with neural branches
- Story: AI connectivity. Your business, networked and intelligent.
- Files: `logo-3-icon.svg`, `logo-3-full.svg`

## Usage

- **Website header:** Full logo (icon + text)
- **App icon / favicon:** Icon only
- **Business card:** Full logo
- **Social media avatar:** Icon only on navy background
- **Watermark:** Icon only, 10% opacity

## favicon

Use concept 2 icon (`logo-2-icon.svg`) as favicon. For 16x16, the shield shape reads clearly even at tiny sizes.
